# 8020REI — Company & Product Reference

---

## What 8020REI Does

8020REI is a premium B2B SaaS platform that provides exclusive, predictive real estate data to professional real estate investors. The company serves investors who rely on high-quality data intelligence to find deals before their competition — primarily wholesalers and fix-and-flip operators doing 50+ deals per year. The platform combines AI-driven targeting, county-level market exclusivity, and managed strategic services under a single subscription (~$2,300-$2,500/month average + fees).

The core business model is a data-to-deals pipeline. 8020REI aggregates property, owner, and distress data from county records, tax assessors, and proprietary sources, then applies each investor's unique targeting criteria (their "BuyBox") to generate monthly lists of high-probability motivated sellers. These lists are delivered ready for outreach across mail, cold calling, SMS, and digital channels.

What makes this different from a data vendor is the intelligence layer. Every client gets AI trained on their own closed deal history, county-level exclusivity that locks out competitors, and a dedicated Customer Success Manager who optimizes targeting monthly. The result: $2.1B+ in deals powered, 1,200+ protected counties, and a 70.8% discovery-to-opportunity conversion rate.

## How We Create Value

The 8020REI value chain follows four stages:

1. **Data Acquisition** — Aggregate property, owner, and distress data from county records, tax assessors, and proprietary sources. This includes probate filings, divorce records, tax liens, code violations, and other distress signals that indicate a motivated seller.

2. **Marketing Application** — Apply the investor's BuyBox filters to generate targeted lists. Each list is optimized for multiple outreach channels (direct mail, cold calling, SMS, digital). Skip tracing appends phone numbers, emails, and other contact info so clients can start outreach immediately.

3. **Lead Conversion** — The investor contacts property owners from the list. Motivated sellers become leads. An MQL is a lead who meets the investor's minimum criteria for motivation, timeline, and price range.

4. **Contract Sale** — Leads convert to contracts, then to closed deals via one of the standard exit strategies: wholesale, wholetail, fix & flip, novation, buy & hold, creative financing, or subject-to.

The **Feedback Loop** accelerates this cycle over time. Clients report monthly results (closed deals, bad contacts, opt-outs), the AI models learn from outcomes and refine targeting, and the next month's lists get sharper. Clients who actively participate see measurably better results.

## Products and Features

### BuyBox

An investor's property targeting criteria — the filter that determines which properties appear in their monthly data delivery. Parameters include price range, property type, size (sqft, bedrooms, lot size), location (counties, zip codes), distress signals (ownership duration, absentee status, tax delinquency), and exclusions (corporate owners, trusts, recently marketed). Configured during onboarding and refined over time using Feedback Loop data.

### BuyBox IQ

Client-specific AI trained on each investor's closed deal history. Unlike competitors who use generic models applied identically to every subscriber, BuyBox IQ learns what works for each individual client — their markets, their deal types, their price points — and improves targeting accordingly.

### Reverse Buy Box

Surfaces the deals that standard distressed-seller filters miss. Traditional BuyBox filtering starts with distress signals (tax liens, probate, absentee owners) and finds matching properties. Reverse Buy Box works the other direction — identifying high-probability opportunities that don't match conventional distress criteria. This accounts for approximately 40% of client revenue. No competitor offers an equivalent feature.

### Hidden Gems

Properties where the year built or last sale date is unknown in public records. Other data vendors skip these because they cannot estimate value accurately. 8020REI's proprietary AVM model can value them, creating an exclusive data advantage. Hidden Gems represent ~40% of 8020REI client revenue historically because these properties have less competition — other vendors' clients never see them.

### AVM (Automated Valuation Model)

8020REI uses a two-tier approach to property valuation:
- **Vendor AVM** — When the third-party vendor's confidence score is 70+, use their automated valuation.
- **Proprietary AVM** — When confidence is low, use 8020REI's weighted regression model based on lot size, building area, bedrooms, bathrooms, year built, and tax assessment value. This is what enables Hidden Gem valuation — it works even when comparable sales data is missing.

### Skip Tracing

The process of appending phone numbers, email addresses, and other contact information to property owner records. 8020REI skip-traces records as part of the data delivery, so clients can immediately begin outreach without purchasing separate skip tracing services.

### Feedback Loop

8020REI's core differentiator — a virtuous cycle where client outcomes improve data quality:
1. **Share** — Client reports monthly results (closed deals, bad contacts, opt-outs)
2. **Optimize** — AI models learn from outcomes, refining targeting criteria
3. **Target** — Next month's data lists are sharper, with higher-probability leads
4. **Close** — Better targeting means higher conversion rates and lower cost per deal

Clients share data via CRM integration (Salesforce, Podio, Left Main REI, REsimpli) or manual Google Sheets upload.

### County Exclusivity

Each county has a limited number of "seats." Once filled, no new clients can subscribe to that market. 8020REI currently protects 1,200+ counties. This is the single most defensible competitive moat — clients pay for data that their competitors literally cannot access.

## Competitive Landscape

The market has 22+ competitors across six categories. Most address one piece of the investor acquisition puzzle — data OR marketing OR CRM OR analytics — while 8020REI combines all of these into a single managed offering.

**Direct Predictive AI (HIGH threat):** Audantic, LeadFlow/ScopeAI, SmartZip, Offrs. These have generic AI models, no county exclusivity at 8020REI's scale, and stale data refresh cycles (quarterly vs. 8020REI's daily).

**Done-for-You Marketing (MEDIUM-HIGH threat):** Investor Machine, Lead Mining Pros. Investor Machine is mail-only with 360 protected counties (vs. 1,200+). Lead Mining Pros has no AI or exclusivity.

**Self-Serve Data Platforms (MEDIUM-HIGH threat):** BatchLeads, PropStream, DealMachine, Prycd. Zero territory exclusivity — every user pulls identical lists. Generic AI scores. DIY tools with no strategic guidance.

**All-in-One REI Platforms (MEDIUM threat):** REsimpli, Realeflow, FreedomSoft, REI Automated. No exclusivity, generic scoring, no managed service. Better positioned as CRMs that 8020REI data feeds into.

**Enterprise Data Infrastructure (LOW-MEDIUM threat):** HouseCanary, ATTOM Data, Cherre, Reonomy. Different ICP entirely — institutional buyers managing $100M+ portfolios, not operators doing 50-500 deals/year.

**Complementary/Adjacent (LOW threat):** InvestorLift (disposition marketplace — partnership opportunity), Launch Control (SMS execution — existing partner), Haines & Co. (legacy directories — not meaningful).
