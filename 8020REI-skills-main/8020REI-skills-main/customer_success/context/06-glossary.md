# 8020REI — CSM Glossary

Business, product, and real estate investment terms relevant to Customer Success operations. Organized alphabetically.

---

**8020REI** — The company. A premium B2B SaaS platform providing exclusive, predictive real estate data to professional real estate investors.

**Absentee Owner** — A property owner whose mailing address differs from the property address. Level 1: in-state but different address. Level 2: out-of-state. Higher absentee levels correlate with higher motivation to sell.

**Action Plan** — A scoring and routing system that determines how to market to each property based on distress signals, equity, and owner profile. Assigns an urgency tier (high/medium/low) and recommends outreach channels.

**AE (Account Executive)** — Sales team member responsible for discovery calls, demos, and closing deals. Distinct from SDR who handles initial outreach.

**ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)** — MRR multiplied by 12. Total annualized subscription revenue.

**AVM (Automated Valuation Model)** — The system used to estimate property market value. 8020REI uses a two-tier approach: vendor AVM when confidence is high (score 70+), proprietary weighted regression model when confidence is low. The proprietary model is what enables Hidden Gem valuation.

**BuyBox** — An investor's specific property targeting criteria: price range, property type, size, location, distress signals, and exclusions. Configured during onboarding and refined over time using Feedback Loop data.

**BuyBox IQ** — Client-specific AI trained on each investor's closed deal history. Unlike competitors' generic models, BuyBox IQ learns what works for each individual client.

**Call Outcome** — The result status of a sales or CS call. Common values: Book Demo, Opportunity, Nurture, Canceled, No Show.

**Churn** — When a client cancels their subscription.

**Client** — An active paying subscriber.

**Client Health Score** — A 1-5 scale health indicator for individual clients, used to assess engagement and retention risk.

**Client Status** — The subscription lifecycle state: Active, Overdue, Paused, Hold, or Canceled.

**County** — A geographic territory that clients subscribe to. Each county has limited "seats" for market exclusivity.

**County Exclusivity** — 8020REI's model of limiting the number of clients per county to protect data value. Currently 1,200+ protected counties. The single most defensible competitive moat.

**CPL (Cost per Lead)** — Total ad spend divided by total leads generated.

**CPMQL (Cost per Market Qualified Lead)** — Total ad spend (Google + Meta) divided by MQL count. Target: under $250 (green), $250-$300 (yellow), over $300 (red).

**Creative Financing** — Exit strategy using seller financing, subject-to deals, or lease options. Requires minimal cash from the investor.

**Deal** — A potential or closed sale in the pipeline.

**Deal Value** — Total first-year contract value: (MRR x 12) + onboarding fee.

**Discovery Call** — First sales call with a qualified lead. Success outcomes: Opportunity, Book Demo.

**Distress Signals** — Indicators that a property owner may be motivated to sell: tax delinquency, pre-foreclosure, probate/estate, code violations, vacancy, high equity with long ownership, absentee ownership.

**Downsizing** — A distress category targeting elderly owners in homes larger than they need. Criteria: 25+ years ownership, 3-5 bedrooms, 1,300+ sqft.

**Engagement Call** — Regular CSM check-in with a client to review results and optimize targeting.

**Expansion** — When a client adds new counties or markets to their subscription.

**Fathom AI** — Meeting recording and transcription tool. Source of call notes and transcripts used for sales and CS call analysis.

**Feedback Loop** — 8020REI's core competitive advantage. A virtuous cycle: client shares results, AI optimizes targeting, next month's lists improve, client closes more deals.

**Fix & Flip** — Exit strategy where the investor buys, renovates, and resells at market value. Higher profit, higher risk than wholesale.

**Fulfillment** — The monthly delivery of fresh data lists to clients based on their BuyBox criteria.

**Funnel Status** — The progression stage of a lead through the sales funnel, from initial contact through conversion to client.

**Hidden Gems** — Properties where the year built or last sale date is unknown in public records. Other vendors skip these; 8020REI's proprietary AVM can value them. Represent ~40% of client revenue.

**Hold** — Temporary account hold at $0/month. Unlike Pause, the client's county exclusivity is NOT protected — their counties may be reassigned. Higher reactivation urgency than Pause.

**ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)** — Professional investors doing 50+ deals/year with $10K+/month marketing spend, a dedicated acquisition manager, and operational readiness within 2 weeks. Investors doing 20+ deals/year are accepted as a gray area — viable clients if properly supported by CS.

**Lead** — A potential customer who has registered interest, or (from the client's perspective) a property owner who has responded to marketing outreach.

**Merlin** — AI chat assistant within the Analytics dashboard, powered by Google Gemini. Answers questions about data, dashboards, and business logic.

**Motivated Seller** — A property owner under pressure to sell, typically exhibiting one or more of the "5 D's": Death, Divorce, Delinquency, Downsizing, Disrepair.

**MQL (Market Qualified Lead)** — A lead qualified as having 20+ known deals/year in target markets.

**MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)** — Monthly subscription revenue from a single client or across all clients.

**Non-Disclosure State** — A state where property sale prices are not publicly recorded, making valuation harder and proprietary data more valuable. Includes: Alaska, Idaho, Kansas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Missouri, Montana, New Mexico, North Dakota, Texas, Utah, Wyoming (12 states).

**Novation** — Exit strategy where the investor markets the property on behalf of the seller and takes a fee at closing.

**NPS (Net Promoter Score)** — Customer satisfaction metric tracked per client.

**Onboarding Fee** — One-time setup fee charged to new clients at the start of their subscription.

**Pause** — Temporary hold on data delivery. Client pays a $500 flat fee per month (regardless of number of markets) to keep their county exclusivity protected. See also: Hold.

**Pipeline** — The sales funnel from lead to closed deal.

**Reverse Buy Box** — A feature that surfaces high-probability deals that standard distressed-seller filters miss. Accounts for approximately 40% of client revenue. No competitor offers an equivalent.

**SDR (Sales Development Representative)** — Team member responsible for initial lead outreach and qualification before handoff to an AE.

**Skip Tracing** — Process of appending phone numbers, email addresses, and other contact info to property owner records. Included as part of 8020REI's data delivery.

**SLA (Service Level Agreement)** — Target response and resolution times for support tickets.

**Subject To** — Exit strategy where the investor takes over existing mortgage payments without formally assuming the loan.

**Valid Lead** — A genuine new prospect, excluding duplicate contacts from existing client companies.

**Wholesale** — The most common exit strategy for 8020REI clients. The investor contracts a property at below-market value and assigns that contract to an end buyer for a fee, never taking ownership.

**Wholetail** — Exit strategy where the investor buys a property, does minimal cleanup (no major rehab), and lists it on the MLS.
